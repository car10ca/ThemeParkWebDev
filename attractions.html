Virtual Reality

Virtual or Reality, we'll let you be the judge. Our VR section is our pride and joy and feature full-motion rides that give you all the acceleration of real-life with all the thrills from beyond your imagination. We have different themes that will surely fit your taste.

Did you ever want to try Snowboarding, Skiing, Surfing, Tennis, Table-Tennis, Football, Basketball, Baseball, Volleyball, Hockey, Ice-Hockey, Rollerblading, Skateboarding? Well, now you can, all without the risk of injury.
