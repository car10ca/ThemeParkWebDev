Welcome to Virtuality

A retro tech theme park like you've never seen before

Let us take you out of this world

Virtuality is the largest theme park based on video gaming and related technology. Here, you'll find unique and immersive state-of-the-art virtual reality experiences, an adult only high-tech casino experience that puts Las Vegas to shame, as well as a trip back to the late 70s / early 80s with a large floor covering all retro games you can think of. But that's not all, take a look at our eSports academy or peek inside our newly built eSports arena where we host competitions every month.

Virtuality is a theme park like no other. We strive to use the most cutting edge technologies to take you on a journey through time and space. You will find escape, horror, excitement and adventure around every corner. Come for a great and experience something you never have before. Take yourself to a completely different world and reality. Virtuality is the best spot for exclusive entertainment.

We offer unique VR experiences from gaming, art, and film to thrill rides, social experiences, and educational content, we've got you covered! Come with friends or just by yourself to Play, Relax and Explore!

Retro is back in fashion

You don't have to be a tech geek to appreciate where we've come from. Let us take you back in time to through the story of gaming, which is a thrill on it's own. Our retro consoles have been revamped and augmented to bring you all the nostalgia with none of the boredom.

Whether you are looking for full-size arcade games, driving games, bar top games, or console games, Virtuality has what you are looking for. We currently have OVER 200 games in stock!

You will feel both young & old

Our park is open to all ages and we have taken great care to make the retro content accessible to the young and our futuristic tech accessible to the not-so-young. All our sections are wheelchair-friendly, allowing mobility to all our visitors. If you have any special needs, our trained staff will go out your way to ensure your experience is comfortable and beyond your wildest dreams.
